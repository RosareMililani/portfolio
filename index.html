About Me

I graduated with a MS in Human-Computer Interaction (Fall '25) at RIT. I also have a Bachelor's degree in New Media Interactive Development (Spring '22). In addition, I was a member and PR Committee Head (Fall '21 - Spring '22) of Women in Computing (WiC), athlete on the RIT Swim & Dive team ('18-'23), ambassador for iSchool and IGM department, teaching assistant (Fall '21 - Spring '23), graduate research assistant (Fall '23 - Spring '24), and member of the Center for Accessibility and Inclusion Research (CAIR).

I am passionate about research and usability to create captivating and accessible user experiences.

Feel free to connect with me via LinkedIn or e-mail!
